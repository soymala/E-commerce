The iPhone 15 Pro Max is a high-end smartphone from Apple. It features a 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display with a resolution of 2796-by-1290 pixels at 460 ppi. The display has rounded corners that follow a beautiful curved design, and these corners are within a standard rectangle. The phone is powered by the A17 Pro chip and runs on iOS. It comes in four colors: Black Titanium, White Titanium, Blue Titanium, and Natural Titanium. The phone has a strong and light aerospace-grade titanium design with a textured matte-glass back. It is available in four storage capacities: 256GB, 512GB, and 1TB. The phone is rated IP68 for splash, water, and dust resistance. The phone has a pro camera system with a 48MP main camera, a 12MP ultra-wide camera, and a 12MP telephoto camera. The phone has a 4422mAh battery capacity.
